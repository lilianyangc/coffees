Coffee drinks are made by brewing hot water with ground coffee beans. The brewing is either done slowly by drip, or done very quickly under pressure by an espresso machine, where the coffee is coined "espresso". Slow-brewed coffees are generally regarded simply as "coffee".
Dripped Coffee
Regular, balanced
Espresso-based Coffee
Concentrated, intense
Some drinks have milk or cream added, steamed milk, plant based milk, foamed milk, flavorings, sweeteners, alcoholic liqueurs added, some are combinations of coffee with espresso or tea.
